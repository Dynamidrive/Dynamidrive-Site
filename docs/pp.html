Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22nd, 2026

Introduction

Dynamidrive ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use the application.

Developer Information:
Dynamidrive is developed and maintained solely by Kai del Castillo. All development, updates, and changes to the App are managed exclusively by Kai del Castillo.

Technology Stack

Dynamidrive is built primarily using Apple's SwiftUI framework for iOS. The App leverages native iOS frameworks and APIs to deliver its functionality, plus selected third-party tools for map rendering, ZIP export/import, and optional on-device audio processing.

Information the App Uses or Stores

1. Location Data

What the App Uses:

  • Real-time GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude)
  • Current speed
  • Altitude
  • Heading/direction
  • Location accuracy information
  • Coarse, shortened map coordinates saved locally only to restore the Mono map background between page changes

How We Use It: Location data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  1. Speed Tracking: To determine your current speed and dynamically adjust the volume of different stems based on how fast you're traveling.

  2. Speed Limit Detection: Your location coordinates are sent to TomTom's "Snap to Roads" API to obtain the current road's speed limit. This allows the App to display whether you're exceeding the posted speed limit.

  3. Map Background Display: When you select "Map" as your background option (instead of "Gradient"), your current location is displayed on a map background in real-time. The default map uses Apple MapKit, and the "Mono" map style uses Mapbox Maps.

  4. Navigation and Display Details: Heading, altitude, speed, and accuracy can be used for speed displays, map positioning, and driving-related interface features.

Important Details:

  • Most location processing is handled by Apple's native CoreLocation and MapKit frameworks
  • Location data is NEVER sent to our servers
  • Location data is NOT shared with third parties except for TomTom speed limit lookups and Mapbox map rendering when the Mono map style is enabled (see Third-Party Services section)
  • The Mono map style reduces the precision of coordinates used for display and throttles map updates to reduce bandwidth
  • You can disable the "Distance Traveled" feature at any time in Settings
  • Location permissions can be revoked at any time through your iOS Settings

2. Distance Traveled Statistics

What is stored in the App:

  • Total distance traveled while a specific soundtrack is playing
  • This data is calculated using GPS coordinates
  • Milestone notification state, such as which distance milestones have already triggered for a soundtrack

Storage and Control:

  • Distance data is stored ONLY on your device using iOS UserDefaults
  • This data is NEVER uploaded to any servers
  • This data is NOT included when you export or share a soundtrack
  • You can disable or delete this data at any time through the App's Settings
  • Disabling "Distance Traveled Statistic" in Settings stops all distance tracking immediately
  • Distance milestone notifications are local notifications generated on your device and can be controlled in Settings

Data Deletion: You can delete all distance tracking data by:

  1. Opening Settings in the App
  2. Scrolling to "Location Privacy"
  3. Tapping "Reset All Distance Data"

3. Audio Files

User-Created Soundtracks:

  • Audio files you import into the app are stored locally on your device
  • Audio files are NEVER automatically uploaded to any server

Audio Separation Feature:

When you use the audio separation feature:

  • You voluntarily select an audio file to process
  • Audio separation happens locally on your device
  • Your selected audio file is not uploaded to Dynamidrive servers for audio separation

4. App Settings and Preferences

What is stored in the App:

  • User interface preferences (colors, backgrounds, sorting options)
  • Audio configuration settings (volume levels, speed thresholds)
  • Feature toggles (audio separation features enabled/disabled, map style preferences)
  • In-app purchase status (subscription and one-time purchase status)
  • Playlist names, playlist membership, selected playlist, and dismissed playlist suggestions
  • Notification preferences for distance milestones and state-entry alerts
  • Shortcut/deep-link state needed for app actions such as opening or controlling Dynamidrive
  • Cached audio separation availability messages downloaded from Dynamidrive's website configuration file

Storage:

  • All preferences are stored locally using iOS UserDefaults and AppStorage
  • Settings are stored on your device only
  • Settings are NOT synced across devices
  • Settings are NOT uploaded to external servers

5. In-App Purchases

What the App Processes:

  • In-app purchases are processed entirely through Apple's StoreKit framework
  • Dynamidrive does NOT collect, store, or have access to your payment information
  • The App only stores a local flag indicating whether you have an active subscription or have made a purchase
  • All payment processing is handled by Apple

Purchase Data:

  • Subscription status is verified through Apple's servers
  • The App receives anonymized transaction information from Apple
  • No credit card or personal payment information is accessible to us

6. Sharing Soundtracks and Playlists

Data Shared:

  • Files uploaded only for the soundtrack(s) that are shared
  • The speed thresholds for those specific files
  • Whether a track uses a track preset or custom value
  • The soundtrack card color associated only with the soundtrack that is shared
  • The soundtrack's name
  • Loop configuration for the shared soundtrack, if the soundtrack contains loops
  • For playlist exports, the playlist name, included soundtrack names, song order, track counts, and the audio files/configuration for the included soundtracks

Data NOT Shared

  • Distance played for the shared soundtrack, or for any soundtrack
  • Any personal information

7. Notifications and Now Playing

Local Notifications:

  • Dynamidrive may send local notifications for audio separation completion and distance milestones
  • Notification text can include the soundtrack name and distance milestone, but notifications are generated on your device and are not sent to Dynamidrive servers
  • You can manage notification permission in iOS Settings and related in-app notification preferences in Settings

Now Playing and Remote Controls:

  • The App uses iOS media playback APIs to show current playback information and support lock-screen, Control Center, and background audio controls
  • Now Playing information can include soundtrack playback status, speed-related display text, and distance values so the app can work while you drive

Data Dynamidrive Does NOT Collect

Dynamidrive does NOT collect or ask for:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • Browsing history
  • Contacts or photos
  • Microphone audio (we do not record or listen to ambient audio)
  • Precise location history (the App only uses real-time location, not historical data)
  • Information about your vehicle
  • Information about your driving habits
  • Advertising profiles or cross-app tracking data

Third-Party Services

TomTom API

Purpose: To retrieve speed limit information for the road you're currently on.

Data Shared:

  • Your current GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude)
  • This data is sent via HTTPS to TomTom's Snap to Roads API

Frequency:

  • Location is sent to TomTom only while the app is in the foreground
  • After an initial speed limit is found, requests are generally throttled to about every 5 location updates while a soundtrack is playing and about every 10 location updates while idle
  • Requests are also skipped when you have not moved significantly from the last request location
  • Requests are throttled to minimize data usage and API calls
  • No speed-limit requests are made while the app is in the background

TomTom's Privacy:

  • TomTom operates under its own privacy policy
  • For more information, visit: TomTom Privacy Policy

Mapbox Maps

Purpose: To display the optional "Mono" map background style.

Data Shared:

  • Mapbox may receive the map tiles, style requests, access token, and coordinate-related requests needed to render the Mono map
  • Dynamidrive truncates and throttles the coordinates it sends into the Mono map view to reduce precision and bandwidth
  • Mapbox operates under its own privacy policy. For more information, visit: Mapbox Privacy Policy

Google Analytics (Website Only)

The Dynamidrive website, including this Privacy Policy page, may use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and page usage. This applies to visits to dynamidrive.app in a web browser, not to app usage inside Dynamidrive.

Apple Services

The App uses the following Apple frameworks and services:

  • MapKit: For displaying maps and processing location data
  • StoreKit: For processing in-app purchases
  • AVFoundation: For audio playback
  • CoreLocation: For GPS and location services
  • UserNotifications: For local audio separation completion and distance milestone notifications
  • MediaPlayer: For lock-screen, Control Center, and Now Playing controls

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

When sharing feedback using Apple's TestFlight service, you share:

  • Device model (e.g. iPhone 17 Pro)
  • iOS version and build number
  • App version and build from TestFlight
  • App Environment (sandbox or production)
  • Crash reports
  • Performance and memory diagnostics
  • System logs related to only Dynamidrive
  • Voluntarily shared screenshots or screen recordings
  • Your written feedback

Who sees this data:

  • The app developer (Kai del Castillo)
  • Apple (only for processing and hosting diagnostics)
  • Other testers and/or developers never see your submissions

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Most user data (soundtracks, settings, playlists, preferences, local distance data, and notification state) is stored locally on your device
  • The App uses iOS standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults, AppStorage, SwiftData where applicable, and the Documents directory)
  • Data persists until you delete the App or manually delete specific content

Audio Processing Storage

  • Audio files selected for audio separation are processed on your device
  • Audio files and separated tracks are not uploaded to Dynamidrive servers for audio processing

Security Measures

  • All network communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Location data sent to TomTom is encrypted in transit
  • Map requests for the optional Mono map are made through Mapbox's SDK and services
  • Audio separation runs locally on your device, so your audio files are not transmitted to Dynamidrive for processing

Your Privacy Rights and Controls

Location Privacy Controls

  1. System-Level Controls:

    • Manage location permissions through iOS Settings > Dynamidrive
    • Choose between "Always Allow," "While Using the App," or "Never"

  2. In-App Controls:

    • Enable/disable the "Distance Traveled Statistic" in App Settings
    • Reset all distance data at any time
    • Choose between Gradient, default Map, and Mono map background behavior in Settings

Data Deletion

You can delete your data by:

  1. Delete Individual Soundtracks:

    • Pressing the Delete button, pressing a soundtrack, and confirming to delete the soundtrack
    • This removes the soundtrack configuration and associated audio files permanently

  2. Delete Distance Data:

    • Settings > Location Privacy > Reset All Distance Data

  3. Delete Playlists:

    • Deleting a playlist removes the local playlist name and membership list
    • Deleting a playlist does not automatically delete the underlying soundtrack audio files unless you delete those soundtracks separately

  4. Delete All App Data:

    • Uninstall the App from your device
    • All local data will be permanently removed
    • All soundtracks made manually or with the Audio Separator will be permanently removed

Export Your Data

  • Soundtracks can be exported as ZIP files containing all audio tracks and configuration
  • Playlists can be exported as ZIP files containing included soundtracks and playlist information
  • Exported files do NOT include distance traveled statistics
  • Shared soundtracks do NOT contain any location or tracking information

Children's Privacy

Dynamidrive does not knowingly ask for personal information from children under 13. The App is intended for users of driving age (typically 16+), but can be also used by passengers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:

  • The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy will be revised
  • Significant changes will be communicated through the App
  • Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

International Users

Dynamidrive can be used anywhere in the world, but it is intended primarily for use in the United States. Your location, including location used outside the United States, is not collected by Dynamidrive or stored as a location history.

Background Location Usage

Why We Need "Always Allow" Permission:

Dynamidrive requests "Always Allow" location permission to enable background functionality:

  • It removes the blue navigation arrow from the status bar or Dynamic Island while background location is active
  • Music volume adjusts based on your speed even when the app is in the background
  • Now Playing information updates with current speed and distance
  • Background audio playback and remote controls continue when appropriate
  • Speed limit checks, map rendering, reverse geocoding, and other foreground-only UI work may pause in the background to reduce battery use and improve stability

Your Control:

  • This permission is entirely optional
  • The App will function with "While Using the App" permission, but background updates will be limited
  • You can change this permission at any time in iOS Settings

Data Retention

  • Location Data: Not retained; used in real-time only
  • Distance Statistics: Stored indefinitely until manually deleted by you
  • Audio Files (Local): Stored until you delete the soundtrack or uninstall the App
  • Audio Files (Audio Processing): Processed on your device and stored locally until you delete them
  • Playlists and App Settings: Stored until you delete them or uninstall the App

Advertising, Analytics, and Tracking

Dynamidrive does NOT use the following inside the iOS app:

  • Third-party advertising networks
  • Third-party app analytics services
  • Crash reporting services except for Apple's TestFlight
  • User behavior tracking
  • Cross-app tracking

The Dynamidrive website may use Google Analytics for website traffic measurement. The App may also make limited service checks to Dynamidrive servers, such as checking whether audio separation is available.

Dynamidrive does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information the App uses, stores, or shares
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: Dynamidrive does not sell personal information)

To exercise these rights, simply uninstall the App to delete all local data, or contact us using the information below.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Access: You can request information about data the App processes
  • Right to Rectification: You can correct inaccurate data (by modifying settings in the App)
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data (by uninstalling the App)
  • Right to Data Portability: You can export your soundtracks using the App's export feature

Legal Basis for Processing:

  • Location data: Consent (you grant location permission) and Contractual Necessity (required for core App functionality)
  • Audio Processing: Consent (you voluntarily select files for on-device processing)
  • Notifications: Consent (you grant notification permission)
  • Website analytics: Legitimate interest in understanding website traffic and improving the website

Summary

What the App uses or stores: Real-time location (for speed, maps, and distance features), audio files you choose to process on device, local app preferences, playlists, and notification state.

What Dynamidrive DOESN'T collect: Personal identifying information, third-party app analytics, advertising data, vehicle information, or permanent location history.

Your control: Full control over location permissions, distance tracking, and all stored data. You can delete this at any time.

Third parties: TomTom may receive coordinates for speed limits, Mapbox may receive map-rendering requests when Mono map is enabled, and Apple handles purchases and system services. Audio separation runs on your device and does not send your audio files to Dynamidrive servers.

Data deletion: Audio files processed by the Audio Separator stay on your device until you delete them. All other local data stays on your device until you delete it.
